German Shorthair Pointer FAQs

What is your breeding philosophy?

We breed ethically and responsibly with the well-being of every German Shorthair Pointer at the center. Our goal is healthy, confident companions that embody the breed's versatility, athleticism, and loyalty.

Can I see the puppy's parents?

Yes. We encourage meeting the parents when possible so you can see their size, temperament, and health firsthand.

What health tests are done?

All of our breeding dogs complete thorough health screenings including hip and elbow evaluations, eye exams, and genetic testing for breed-specific concerns before pairing.

What vaccines and deworming are given?

Every puppy leaves with age-appropriate vaccinations and deworming. You'll receive a complete health record for your vet.

What food do you feed?

We start our GSP puppies on high-quality, protein-rich nutrition suited for active breeds and provide feeding guidelines for a smooth transition at home.

How are puppies socialized?

Puppies grow up in our family home, experiencing kids, adults, and household routines so they become confident, well-adjusted companions.

Are there upcoming litters?

Yes. We keep a waiting list for future German Shorthair Pointer littersâ€”reach out early to reserve a spot and get updates.

Can you share references?

Absolutely. We're happy to connect you with Frostline Seekers families who can speak to their experience and ongoing support.

What training and care should I expect?

GSPs thrive with plenty of exercise, mental stimulation, and positive training. We'll share guidance on socialization, field training basics, and daily routines to set you up for success.
